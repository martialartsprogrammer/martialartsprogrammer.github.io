My Start in Martial Arts

My life started when I started Martial Arts entering High School in the 9th grade, before then, I would practice alone with no guidance, I would practice for about an hour everyday after school, I didn't start practice due to the movies but I started due to personal interest, My first art was Chinese Martial Arts "Kung Fu", i did learn a little Wushu and Tai Chi during my Kung Fu practice. but Kung Fu was my main focus. Tai Chi has taught me valuable things for practice and Wushu was very demanding, learning how to use every part of your body, some may call it a workout there are applications in Martial Arts, it's not there to look pretty....Welcome to my Martial Arts Blog

Thoughts on Striking

My practice has only been Striking, I am not a big person at all, That's the reason why i dont focus on grappling if i were to be in a self defense situation, I would play dirty, if I would try to wrestle with someone who weighs more than me which is very likely, I wouldn't do well.

A short College story...

Entering college, I have met someone who did Karate and Martial Arts Tricking..While my focus wasn't either, I have no experience in Karate or Tricking, it has only been other traditional Martial Arts, Tae Kwon Do and Chinese Martial Arts and Muay Thai, I was invited to visit a Tricking gym on its grand oppening, I did that, and i showed the guys there my martial arts, they all were impressed and told me to keep practicing, I should have a video of me showing them a little Wushu but if I do i can save that for another time, This was the first tricking gym I have ever been to and it was a good experience, being surrounded by others doing Martial Arts and with a thing for it... there's nothing else like it...A short college story.
