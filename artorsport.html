Martial Art or Martial Sport?

Are the arts gone?

They say it's no longer a Martial Art but a Martial Sport, That the arts are history..

My thoughts on the arts vs the sport

Martial Arts just may have gotten popular when Bruce Lee was around, Bruce Lee is about Martial Art's most popular figure. Martial Arts is more than fighting, my thought is "Why fight when you don't have to"? One shouldn't practice to come into a training hall and beat the snout out of someone and leave, I do not practice for that. I practice to perfect the art. I don't practice for sport but I practice to fill a void, something that means alot to me. Not everyone has the same answer as me but one shouldn't *have* to practice to compete commercially. The arts were a pure thing that has gotten exposure and now is known as a UFC or ONE type practice.
